The NAF Story

From a very young age, I carried a deep passion for teaching. Over the years, this passion grew stronger as I gained extensive industry and training experience, and I constantly searched for the right opportunity to turn this dream into reality.

I have always believed that education is one of the most powerful identities an individual can possess. It should never be limited by age, financial background, or circumstances. Learning must be accessible, inclusive, and empowering for everyone.

The journey of NAF became possible because of the guidance and inspiration of my mentor - a true gentleman who not only shaped my professional outlook but also inspired me to contribute meaningfully to society. His values and vision played a crucial role in the foundation of NAF.

NAF was born with a simple yet powerful purpose: to break barriers in education and create opportunities for lifelong learning.

"I invite you to be a part of this journey. Whether you are here to learn or to collaborate, your presence strengthens the NAF mission."
